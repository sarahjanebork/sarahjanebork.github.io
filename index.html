About Me

Oh hi, my name's SJ! I'm an assistant professor at the University of Georgia. I am a primary faculty member in the Engineering Education Transformations Institute and a faculty in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering. My research focuses on engineering graduate student mental health using mixed methods. I earned my PhD and masters degrees in engineering education at the University of Michigan and my bachelor's and master's degrees in electrical engineering at the Ohio State University.
